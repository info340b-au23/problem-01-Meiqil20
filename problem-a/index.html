SpongeBob SquarePants

Description

SpongeBob SquarePants is the titular character of the animated television series "SpongeBob SquarePants."
He is a lovable and energetic sea sponge who resides in a pineapple house under the sea in the fictional city of
Bikini Bottom. SpongeBob is characterized by his unrelenting positivity and an insatiable thirst for adventure.
He possesses an innocent and childlike nature, making him incredibly endearing to both children and adults.
